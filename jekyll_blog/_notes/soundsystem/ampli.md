# Modes
[Great vid](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=2eKHQQRLpW0)

## Stereo mode
2 channels, working separately, L / R. 

## Parallel mode
One source to two speakers. Mono signal. 
I don't know what is the point of this (no power advantage I think), it's a question of phase and overall tuning I thinks, to check. 

## Bridged mono
Connect the speaker between the two positives.  
Double the voltage, so four times the power. 
Basically it's linking the two channels into a big one.
