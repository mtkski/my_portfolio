Loop_design

Concept

The idea is to have a few visuals that rotate during the night, themselves changing in time + audioreactive (alternate logos, intensity, colors).

The visuals are supposed to go in the chill zone, separated from the dancefloor by a big drape. The idea is to have a video stream of the stage with overlays and effects to keep some way of connexion and add a visual mood to the party. The visuals could change colors or intensity during the night, by plugging a notion of time. Making it audioreactive for more interesting and dynamic results, make it dance with the drops.
